Vinyl Redesign

The Vinyl Redesign project was a creative journey to breathe new life into Louis Tomlinson's "Walls" album. This exciting venture involved a transformation of the album's entire packaging, including the front and back covers, side panels, inner artwork, and the vinyl disc itself. My goal was to capture the soul of Louis Tomlinson's music and translate it into a visual language that resonated with fans and new listeners alike, fostering a deeper connection between the artist and their audience. Completed in May 2022 and meticulously crafted with Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop, this project encapsulates a harmonious blend of art and music.

Vision Board

Utilized the interplay of greyscale and bright tones of orange, mirroring the artist's distinctive color palette. This striking contrast not only pays homage to Louis Tomlinson's signature style but also heightened the visual impact of the album's packaging. Moreover, I integrated different patterns to introduce texture and depth across the entire vinyl and its accompanying elements, further elevating the tactile and sensory experience for fans and collectors. This design approach was crafted to create an immersive connection between the music and its visual representation, offering a captivating and harmonious experience that resonated with the essence of the album.
